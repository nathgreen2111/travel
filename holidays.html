Our holidays

The trips we know inside out

Four kinds of holiday we plan and book all the time, because they are the ones we love taking ourselves. Pick the one that sounds like you, or mix them into one trip.

Theme parks

From the gentle first timer worries to proper ride strategy, we plan park days that flow. We know where the queues build, when to eat, and which moments are worth slowing down for.

What we sort for you
  • Park tickets and the right number of days
  • Hotel close to the action, or quieter if you prefer
  • A loose day by day plan, not a military schedule
  • The rides and shows actually worth your time

Orlando & Disney

This is our second home. We are Disney Vacation Club members with our home resort at Disney's Riviera, out in Orlando at least three times a year, and we have experienced two Disney VIP tours. We genuinely know the property.

Where we come into our own
  • Resort choice, on property or off, to suit your trip
  • Disney and Universal park strategy that works
  • Dining reservations and the spots worth booking
  • Add ons like Memory Maker, VIP tours and extras

Escape room trips

Nathan has played over 138 escape rooms and designs them for a living, so this is not a guess. We build trips around the best rated rooms in a city, and weave in everything else worth doing while you are there.

What a room trip includes
  • The top rated rooms, booked at the right times
  • A city plan around the games, not just the games
  • Food and downtime built in so it never feels rushed
  • Honest picks, we will tell you which rooms to skip

City breaks

Our favourite kind of long weekend. Fast, full European city breaks that pack in a theme park, an escape room or two, and the food and sights that make a place worth visiting.

How we shape a city break
  • Flights and a hotel in the right neighbourhood
  • A realistic plan that still leaves room to wander
  • The right hire car when you need one, sorted properly
  • Parks, rooms, dining and the little local gems
