How it started

It started with a locked door

We met in an escape room. Since then we have been chasing that same feeling everywhere we go, the click of a puzzle solving, the drop of a coaster, the quiet on Main Street just before the fireworks.

Orlando has a special place in our hearts. We are Disney Vacation Club members with our home at the Riviera Resort, out there at least three times a year, and we have been lucky enough to experience two Disney VIP tours. We know the property, the resorts and the small details that make a trip.

Between us we have played over 138 escape rooms across Amsterdam, Belgium and beyond, and next year we are heading to Canada to play the current world number one. Nathan also designs and runs his own escape rooms, so puzzles are not just a hobby, they are the job.

When we are not in a park, you will find us on a fast city break somewhere in Europe, building it around the things we love most. Now we help other people do the same.
